Course Examinations
========================================

This section contains comprehensive information about all course examinations, including schedules, procedures, and preparation materials. **The Summer 2026 STAT 35000-003 section is fully online and asynchronous; all three exams are proctored online via Respondus LockDown Browser with webcam monitoring.**

General Exam Policies
------------------------------------------------------------------

.. admonition:: Exam Day Essentials
   :class: important

   For all exams:

   - **Exams are administered online in Brightspace using Respondus LockDown Browser** with webcam and audio monitoring.
   - Each exam is open for a **24-hour window**; once you begin, you have a fixed time limit (80 minutes for Exam 1 and Exam 2; 140 minutes for the Final).
   - **One attempt only** — the exam cannot be paused, exited, and restarted.
   - **Purdue photo ID** required for identity verification before the exam starts.
   - **No breaks** during the exam. Leaving the camera view will be flagged.
   - **Complete the practice quiz in Brightspace** before your first exam to verify your setup.
